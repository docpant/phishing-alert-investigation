# Ticket Comments

The phishing alert was investigated according to the phishing incident response playbook.

The email contained a suspicious executable attachment named bfsvc.exe and originated from an untrusted sender. Review of the provided SHA256 hash confirmed the attachment is known malicious. Multiple phishing indicators were present, including grammatical errors, sender inconsistencies, and social engineering techniques.

The ticket status was updated to Escalated because the attachment was determined to be malicious and presents a risk to organizational systems.
